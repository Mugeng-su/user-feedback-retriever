# Demo Script

## English Version

### 30-second Pitch

This is an AI prototype for marketing and growth teams that review creator-made product videos. Instead of only summarizing a video, it first decides whether the video is worth learning from, then turns transcript and comment evidence into a structured report: why the content worked, what user language can be reused, and what the next content test should explore.

### 3-minute Walkthrough

1. Open the home page.
2. Say: "The problem is that teams can see views and comments, but they still struggle to understand what is reusable."
3. Show the four input steps.
4. Explain: "The tool needs both performance signals and text evidence. It should not invent insights when evidence is weak."
5. Click the strong demo sample card or open an existing strong sample result.
6. Start with the decision panel.
7. Show the two report blocks: Video Analysis and Language Asset Reuse.
8. Show the reusable brief export.
9. Click the screened-out demo sample card or open an existing weak sample result.
10. Explain: "This is an intentional product decision. Weak or low-evidence material should stop early."

### What To Emphasize

- The value is not generic summarization.
- The value is workflow design: screening, evidence assessment, content mechanics, and reusable language.
- The strongest product decision is refusing to over-analyze weak inputs.
- The output is designed for a marketing team to reuse, not just read.

### Good Closing Line

The prototype is intentionally narrow: YouTube product videos with transcript and comment evidence. That narrowness makes the AI output more trustworthy and easier to evaluate.

## 中文版本

### 30 秒介绍

这是一个面向营销和增长团队的 AI 原型，用来复盘创作者制作的产品视频。它不只是总结视频内容，而是先判断这条视频是否值得学习，再把字幕和评论证据转化成结构化报告：内容为什么有效、哪些用户语言可以复用、下一轮内容测试应该探索什么方向。

### 3 分钟演示流程

1. 打开首页。
2. 说明：“问题是团队虽然能看到播放和评论，但仍然很难判断哪些内容和表达真的值得复用。”
3. 展示四步输入流程。
4. 说明：“这个工具同时需要表现信号和文本证据。证据不足时，它不应该硬编洞察。”
5. 点击强样本 demo 卡片，或打开一条已有强样本结果。
6. 先从决策面板讲起。
7. 展示两个报告大区：视频分析和语言资产复用。
8. 展示可复用 brief 导出。
9. 点击弱素材 demo 卡片，或打开一条已有弱样本结果。
10. 说明：“这是一个有意做出的产品判断。表现弱或证据不足的素材应该提前停止，而不是强行完整分析。”

### 展示时要强调什么

- 这个产品的价值不是泛泛总结。
- 真正价值在于工作流设计：素材筛选、证据判断、内容机制和可复用语言。
- 最强的产品判断是拒绝过度分析弱输入。
- 输出不是为了让人读完就结束，而是为了让营销团队带回下一轮内容和文案生产。

### 好用的收尾句

这个原型有意做得很窄：只聚焦有字幕和评论证据的 YouTube 产品视频。这个窄范围让 AI 输出更可信，也更容易评估。
